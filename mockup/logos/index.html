Design Guidelines

  • Minimum Size: Do not use the icon smaller than 32x32 pixels to maintain clarity
  • Clear Space: Maintain at least 15px of clear space around the logo
  • Background Colors: Logo works on light, dark, and gradient backgrounds
  • Text Pairing: Always use "LankaConnect" as one word in Arial or Helvetica Bold
  • Primary Text Color: Maroon (#8B1538) on light backgrounds, White on dark backgrounds
  • Do Not: Modify the logo colors, distort proportions, or add effects
  • File Format: Use PNG for web, ensure high resolution for print (300 DPI minimum)

Logo Symbolism

  • Heritage Lotus: Overlapping circles forming a lotus pattern represent growth, enlightenment, and Sri Lankan heritage
  • Sri Lankan Lion: The golden lion with sword symbolizes strength, courage, and national pride from the Sri Lankan flag
  • Flag Colors: Saffron (orange), maroon (red), and green circles represent the Sri Lankan flag's vibrant colors
  • Community Connection: The circular design represents unity and bringing the diaspora community together
