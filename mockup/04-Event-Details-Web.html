üìñ About This Event

Join us for the most spectacular Sinhala New Year celebration in Ohio! This year, we're bringing together the entire Sri Lankan American community for an unforgettable day of traditions, games, food, and fellowship.


This family-friendly event celebrates the rich cultural heritage of Sri Lanka with authentic traditions passed down through generations. Whether you're a long-time community member or new to our beautiful culture, everyone is welcome!


The Sri Lankan Association of Cleveland has been organizing this annual celebration for over 15 years, making it the largest gathering of Sri Lankan Americans in the Midwest region.

‚ú® Event Highlights
  • Traditional Games - Pillow fights on a log, breaking clay pots, tug of war, and many more nostalgic games that bring back childhood memories
  • Authentic Cuisine - Kiribath, kavum, kokis, aluwa, and a full traditional lunch buffet with rice, curry, and accompaniments
  • Cultural Performances - Kandyan dancing, drumming ensembles, and vocal performances by community artists
  • Kids Activities - Face painting, bouncy castle, traditional dress photo booth, and prizes for game winners
  • New Year Rituals - Boiling of milk, lighting of the hearth, anointing with oil, and other auspicious customs
  • Live Entertainment - DJ playing baila and popular Sri Lankan music throughout the day
